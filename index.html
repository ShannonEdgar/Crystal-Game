Welcome to Crystal Collector

There are 4 crystals below. Clicking each will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the target number, you loose the game if your total score goes above the target number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
